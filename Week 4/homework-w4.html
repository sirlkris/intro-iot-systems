Node $A$ acts basically as a digital logic gate for nodes $B-E$, it gets high (1) or low (0) signal and outputs high (1) or low (0) signal. Two $or$ gates and one $and$ gate. So either node $B$ or $D$ (temperature greater than 25 C) and node $C$ or $E$ (wind speed greater than 5 m/s) need to be high to node $A$ to output a high signal, otherwise it is low.
